An open-source, API-first knowledge extraction engine that converts earnings calls, SEC filings, press releases, and research reports into queryable knowledge graphs.

Financial analysts dedicate hours reading reports to understand the causal relationships driving revenue, risk, and growth. MarketFoundry treats unstructured text as a first-class data source, automatically building custom knowledge graphs that answer "Why did Q3 miss?" or "What caused churn?" with evidence from across your entire document corpus.

The Problem We Solve

The Challenge

Traditional analysts spend hours manually reading through filings, transcripts, and reports to piece together what's driving revenue, risk, and growth. It's slow, it doesn't scale, and it keeps valuable context out of the tools that actually make decisions. The firms building the next generation of financial analytics know that unstructured text can't stay unstructured. They're building RAG systems, analyst dashboards, and data pipelines ‚Äî but without structured extraction upstream, documents still go in as raw text and insight still has to come out through manual effort. Market Foundry changes that. Open-source and API-first, we give data-driven teams the extraction layer their pipelines are missing ‚Äî so the narrative behind the numbers becomes just as queryable as the numbers themselves.

Our Solution: Document-aware extraction, built for modern financial pipelines

Market Foundry classifies each document, sections it intelligently, and routes it to specialized schemas that extract entities, relationships, and where present, causal structure. The result is a unified knowledge graph your existing tools can actually work with. Same RAG system, sharper retrieval. Same dashboard, richer context. Built open-source so you can integrate it, extend it, and make it your own.

How It Works

MarketFoundry uses a document-aware pipeline that intelligently processes each financial document type to extract the most valuable causal relationships.

1

Document Ingestion

Multi-format financial documents normalized into structured text.

2

Query, RAG, Analytics

Query directly, plug into RAG workflows, or feed analytics pipelines.

1

Document Ingestion

Upload PDF, DOCX, HTML, or JSON files. Market Foundry handles any financial document format; earnings calls, SEC filings, press releases, research reports, and news articles.

2

Document Classification

An ensemble ML model automatically detects document type and routes it to the appropriate extraction schema. No manual configuration needed.

3

Intelligent Sectioning

LLMs extract topics dynamically and auto-generate YAML templates tailored to each section, preserving the context that generic text chunking would lose.

4

Schema Routing

Each section is matched to a specialized extraction schema based on its topic, ensuring the right relationships get captured for the right content.

5

Extraction

OneKE + Qwen2.5-7B extracts structured triples; subject, predicate, object, with source citations so every insight is traceable back to its document.

6

Knowledge Graph

All triples are stored in Neo4j, creating a unified graph across your entire document corpus. Query it directly, plug it into your RAG system, or pipe it into your analytics stack.
